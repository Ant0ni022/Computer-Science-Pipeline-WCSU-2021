Moog

Moog synthesizers were created by Dr. Robert Moog under the name Moog Music. Popular models include the Moog Modular, Minimoog, Moog Rogue and Moog Source.

ARP

ARP Instruments Inc was set up by Alan Pearlman, and was the main competitor for Moog during the 1970s. Popular models include the Arp 2600 and the Arp Odyssey.
