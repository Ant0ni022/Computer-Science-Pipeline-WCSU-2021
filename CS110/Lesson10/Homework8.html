Ushering in 2021 with the wildest weather on record

Bethel, CT The new year begun at midnight and with dawned our hopes for an interesting year. As we bid farewell to 2020, we
have no regrets at its passing. We hope that the boring sameness that characterized that tedious year will never be repeated. The
quiet calm demeanor of politicians as they carried out their tasks of governing , the dignified and respectful reporting from the
media, the silent advance of peace throughout the world, the exemplary behavior of leaders from the worlds of entertainment and
industry...pardon us as we stifle yet another yawn...

At the very least the weather of the last few days has provided some welcome relief from the ubiquitous boredom
The temperatures observed in the Northeast have been some of the very highest on record. The revelers in Times Square in New York
City certainly appreciated being able to doff coats and hats for shorts and tank tops as they awaited the 73rd annual dropping of
the Tiffany ball.Is it reported that sales of sodas and other cold drinks topped expectations by a substantial margin In Russia,
enthusiasts usher & in the new year with an impromptu sailboat race on the Neva ver In northern Japan, sun bathers crowded
beaches and atop Mount Everest a hip-hop dance parry reverberated until the wee hours
